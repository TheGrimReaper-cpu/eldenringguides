More About Malenia

Malenia, Blade of Miquella, is an optional boss in Elden Ring. She is infamous for her ability to heal with every successful attack and her swift, deadly combos.

Malenia's Weaknesses

  • Fire Damage: Weapons or spells with fire effects deal significant damage to her.
  • Bleed Effects: Bleed weapons can trigger blood loss, making her more vulnerable.
  • Poise Damage: Heavy weapons like greatswords or colossal weapons can break her stance.
